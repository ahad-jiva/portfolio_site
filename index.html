About Me

I'm an undergraduate student at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo studying applied mathematics and computer science with a strong interest in algorithmic problem-solving, machine learning, and applications of advanced theoretical mathematics to real-world problems. I am fluent in Python, C/C++, and Java, and have experience with libraries such as TensorFlow and PyTorch.
